About AKGEC

Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College (AKGEC), affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, is a premier institution that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development. Established with a commitment to providing world-class technical education, AKGEC has consistently ranked among the top private engineering colleges in India.

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in key engineering disciplines, with a strong emphasis on industry-driven curriculum, practical exposure, and research-based learning. Our departments are equipped with modern laboratories, smart classrooms, and access to digital learning resources to facilitate experiential learning.

Beyond academics, AKGEC nurtures leadership, ethics, and communication through various student-run societies, technical fests, innovation challenges, and national-level events. This environment not only enhances professional skills but also instills confidence and teamwork among students.

The institution maintains active collaborations with reputed industries and R&D organizations such as Siemens, TCS, and IBM. These partnerships help bridge the gap between academia and industry, and offer certification programs, internships, and real-time project experience to our students.

The Training and Placement Cell is a dedicated unit that ensures every student receives rigorous pre-placement training including aptitude, soft skills, technical workshops, and mock interviews. Our consistent placement records and growing list of recruiters reflect our focus on producing employable and industry-ready graduates.

Why Recruit from AKGEC

  • Highly qualified faculty from top institutions
  • Robust academic and research infrastructure
  • Students trained in real-time projects and soft skills
  • Strong alumni network across industries
  • Multiple MOUs with industry leaders
